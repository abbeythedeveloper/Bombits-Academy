About

BomBits Academy

In today's increasingly digitized world, having digital skills is essential for success in almost any field. BomBits offers one year of hands-on training in ICT and multimedia, web development, and web and graphic design skills to teenagers and young adults (whose families cannot afford a university education and who make less than 30,000 naira per month). BomBits fights poverty and spurs economic growth. More than ever, it is essential to teach the next generation the skills they need to become competent designers and developers in the thriving ICT sector.

The Bits Academy program helps people develop as freelancers and business owners. In addition, the instruction includes practical life skills that aid the children in overcoming their most pressing problems,, young people can learn a wide range of digital skills, including coding, web design. etc.

Mission Statement To foster the advancement of both male and female technologists, business owners, and leaders. The internet, graphic and online design, web development, and filmmaking are all areas where youth are making strides..

Vision Statement To create a more prosperous, fair, and inclusive Nigerian society where youths are empowered to pursue their goals by granting them access to decent employment, high-quality education, and skill-building opportunities.
